About Us

Our Story

The Fabled Nectar was established in 1942 by the Fable brothers on a farm owned by their family. Honey runs in their blood and the conservation of bees has been their objective from the very beginning.

Our Brand

Our brand is designed to evoke nostalgia and sophistication, appealing to those who appreciate artisanal craftsmanship and natural remedies. Presented in vintage–inspired glass jars with ornate labels, The Fabled Nectar reflects the charm of an old-world apothecary. It blends the ancient art of apothecary with the sweet allure of nature. Specializing in handcrafted, infused honeys, our products are thoughtfully curated to offer both flavor and wellness benefits. Each jar is infused with carefully selected botanicals, spices, or citrus, creating unique combinations like vanilla marshmallow, cinnamon clove, and strawberry peach.

Bee Friendly Sanctuary Fund

At The Fabled Nectar, we believe in the preservation of bees. A portion of every sale is donated to the Bee Friendly Sanctuary Fund, which focuses on restoring wildflower planting and sustainable beekeeping programs.
